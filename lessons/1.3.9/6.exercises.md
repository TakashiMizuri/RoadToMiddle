> Complete after lectures and passing `4.test-yourself.md`.

## Easy

### E1. Before and after Count
**Goal:** Observe lazy evaluation.
**Steps:** `int[] data`; `Where` with counter in predicate; print counter before/after `query.Count()`.
**Success criteria:** Written observation matches code.

### E2. Double foreach
**Goal:** Multiple enumeration.
**Steps:** `File.ReadLines` or mock `IEnumerable` that logs on each element read. Two `foreach` on same query.
**Success criteria:** Log proves two passes; fix with `ToList()`.
