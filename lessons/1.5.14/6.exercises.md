# Exercises: ArrayPool

## Easy
### E1. Rent/Return wrapper
**Goal:** Method copies stream chunk to pooled buffer; Return in finally.
**Success criteria:** clearArray documented choice.

## Medium
### M2. Leak demo
**Goal:** Rent 1000 without Return — observe behavior; fix.
**Success criteria:** Explanation in comment.

## Hard
### H1. Compare alloc rate
**Goal:** Loop 100k: `new byte[1024]` vs pool; compare GC.GetTotalMemory (rough).
**Success criteria:** One paragraph result.
