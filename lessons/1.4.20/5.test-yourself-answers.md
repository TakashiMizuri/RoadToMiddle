## Answers

### 1. [Recall]
**Answer:** Синхронный код **блокирует** thread, **ожидая** async операцию (`.Result`, `.Wait()`, etc.).

---

### 2. [Recall]
**Answer:** Blocked thread **не обслуживает** pool; continuation async work нужен **свободный** thread → **starvation/deadlock** under load.

---

### 3. [Understanding]
**Answer:** **`Task.Run`** занимает pool thread на **blocking disk I/O**; **`ReadAllBytesAsync`** освобождает thread на I/O (true async).

---

### 4. [Understanding]
**Answer:** Если **`GetAsync`** async, **все callers** (service, controller) must become **`async Task`** — иначе где-то появится **`.Result`**.

---

### 5. [Application]
**Answer:** **`public async Task<IActionResult> Get()`** + **`return Ok(await _svc.GetAsync());`**

---

### 6. [Application]
**Answer:** **Sync-over-async** на каждом request в middleware — **multiplies starvation**; use **`InvokeAsync`** + **await _next**.

---

### 7. [Analysis]
**Answer:** Still **blocks thread inside Task.Run**; не масштабирует I/O; **скрывает** need for true async API; **2x thread** pressure.

---

### 8. [Analysis]
**Answer:** **Same blocking semantics** for pool — оба sync block on incomplete task.

---

### 9. [Understanding]
**Answer:** **CPU-bound** offload with **throttling** — не для masquerading I/O.

---

### 10. [Recall]
**Answer:** **Classic deadlock** — UI thread blocked waiting async that posts continuation back to UI thread.

---

### 11. [Application]
**Answer:** **Background queue/worker** (202 Accepted) или **dedicated sync service** — not **`.Result`** in request.

---

### 12. [Understanding]
**Answer:** Ctor sync; **`.Result`** blocks startup thread / risks deadlock; use **factory/hosted init**.

---

### 13. [Analysis]
**Answer:** **Sync enumeration** during serialization / unexpected blocking query on request thread.

---

### 14. [Application]
**Answer:** **`.Result`**, **`.Wait()`**, **`GetAwaiter().GetResult()`** (and sync-over-async middleware).

---

### 15. [Understanding]
**Answer:** **Нет** — only affects **where continuation runs**; still need **await**, not **`.Result`**.
