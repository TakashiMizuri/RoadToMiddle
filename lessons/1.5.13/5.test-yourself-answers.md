# Answers: stackalloc

### 1. **Span<byte>** (in safe context).
### 2. When **method returns** (stack frame popped).
### 3. **Accumulated** stack use / overflow per iteration frame.
### 4. stackalloc **no GC**; `new` **heap** + GC pressure.
### 5. **ArrayPool** — 4 KB large for typical stack budget per call.
### 6. **No** — use-after-free for caller.
### 7. **Bad** — unbounded → StackOverflow / attack.
### 8. Allocates block on **stack** in IL.
### 9. **stackalloc 32** or even stack locals; pool if many concurrent.
### 10. **Yes StackOverflow** — stack frames + alloc sum.
