# Exercises: Func, Action, Predicate

> Complete after passing `4.test-yourself.md`.

## Easy

### E1. LINQ Filters
**Goal:** Given `List<Product>`, use `Where` with `Func<Product,bool>` for price range and category.
**Success criteria:** Two separate filters; no custom delegate types.

### E2. Method Group
**Goal:** Pass `DateTime.ToString` (with format) or `int.ToString` as `Func<,>` to a helper `ApplyAll`.
**Success criteria:** Method group conversion compiles.

## Medium

### M1. Callback Processor
**Goal:** `void ForEach<T>(IEnumerable<T> items, Action<T> action, Action<Exception>? onError = null)` — invoke with error handling.
**Success criteria:** Uses Action, not Func, for side-effect callback.

### M2. Factory Registration
**Goal:** Register `Func<IServiceProvider, IEmailSender>` in pseudo-DI; resolve and call.
**Success criteria:** Demonstrates Func as factory delegate.

## Hard (stretch)

### H1. Predicate Migration
**Goal:** Refactor `FindAll(Predicate<T>)` call site to LINQ `Where` with `Func<T,bool>`; document equivalence.
**Success criteria:** Same results, modern style.
