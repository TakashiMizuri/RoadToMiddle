## Easy

### E1. Countdown
**Goal:** Practice `while`.

**Steps:**
1. Console app `Loops.Countdown`.
2. Start `int n = 10`; while `n >= 0`, print `n` and decrement.
3. After loop print `"Liftoff!"`.

**Success criteria:** Prints 10 through 0 inclusive, then liftoff.
