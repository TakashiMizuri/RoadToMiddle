## Answers

### 1. [Recall]
**Answer:** **`System.Threading.Channels`** (built-in, NuGet не нужен с .NET Core 3+).

---

### 2. [Recall]
**Answer:** **`ChannelWriter<T>`**, **`ChannelReader<T>`**.

---

### 3. [Understanding]
**Answer:** Когда buffer full, **`WriteAsync`** **await'ит**, пока consumer не освободит slot — producer **замедляется**.

---

### 4. [Understanding]
**Answer:** **Memory growth** без limit; consumer lag → **OOM**; нет backpressure на producers.

---

### 5. [Understanding]
**Answer:** Сигнал **«больше не будет записей»**; после drain reader завершает **`ReadAllAsync`**.

---

### 6. [Application]
**Answer:** **11-й `WriteAsync` suspend'ится** (await), пока reader не прочитает элемент — не блокирует thread, async wait.

---

### 7. [Application]
**Answer:** **`await foreach (var x in channel.Reader.ReadAllAsync(ct))`**.

---

### 8. [Analysis]
**Answer:** Любые два: **unbounded memory**, **no backpressure**, **global coupling**, **lost work on restart** (in-memory), **hard to test**.

---

### 9. [Analysis]
**Answer:** **Multiple producers**, **decoupled timing**, нужен **buffer** между stages — channel; IAsyncEnumerable — single pull iterator.

---

### 10. [Recall]
**Answer:** **`Wait`**, **`DropWrite`**, **`DropOldest`** (и **`DropNewest`** в некоторых версиях — если упомянул, OK).

---

### 11. [Understanding]
**Answer:** Channel **full** (bounded), **completed**, или **canceled**.

---

### 12. [Application]
**Answer:** Writer: **`Complete()`** (stop accepting); Consumer: **drain** `ReadAllAsync` with **cancellation timeout** on host stop.

---

### 13. [Understanding]
**Answer:** **Нет** — in-process only; нужен message broker.

---

### 14. [Analysis]
**Answer:** Acceptable: **metrics/sampling** где старые события менее важны. Not acceptable: **payments, orders**, audit compliance.

---

### 15. [Application]
**Answer:** **Queue depth**, **write wait duration**, **reject/drop rate**, **consumer lag**, **processing errors**.
