## Answers

### 1. [Recall]
**Answer:** **Set-Cookie** (server sets); **Cookie** (client sends back).

---

### 2. [Recall]
**Answer:** **HttpOnly**.

---

### 3. [Recall]
**Answer:** **SameSite=None** (browsers reject None without Secure).

---

### 4. [Understanding]
**Answer:** CSRF relies on browser **automatically sending** cookie on qualifying requests; HttpOnly only blocks **JS read**, not send on cross-site POST/navigation (depending on SameSite).

---

### 5. [Understanding]
**Answer:** **Нет** (typically) — cross-site **subresource/XHR** with Lax **does not** include cookie.

---

### 6. [Understanding]
**Answer:** **Да** — top-level **GET navigation** cross-site allowed under **Lax**.

---

### 7. [Application]
**Answer:** **`SameSite=None`** and **`Secure`** (plus CORS Allow-Credentials and CSRF strategy).

---

### 8. [Application]
**Answer:** **`HttpOnly; Secure; SameSite=Lax`** (typical baseline).

---

### 9. [Application]
**Answer:** **Max-Age** — relative seconds, less clock skew ambiguity than absolute Expires.

---

### 10. [Analysis]
**Answer:** **B** неверно — **Secure** cookies **not sent** over HTTP (except localhost dev quirks).

---

### 11. [Analysis]
**Answer:** **XSS** can read token via `document.cookie` → theft and account takeover.

---

### 12. [Understanding]
**Answer:** Меньший scope → cookie **не отправляется** на лишние paths/subdomains → меньше exposure при XSS/CSRF/leak.

---

### 13. [Analysis]
**Answer:** **SameSite** blocks cross-site cookie; **CORS** must allow credentials + specific Origin; possibly missing **None; Secure** on cookie; browser unlike Postman doesn't auto-ignore SameSite.

---

### 14. [Understanding]
**Answer:** **Cookie** — browser **automatically attaches** on matching requests; **Bearer** — developer **explicitly** sets Authorization header (no automatic cross-site send by default).

## Model answers for practical

```
Set-Cookie: tok=1; Path=/; Secure; SameSite=None
```

(HttpOnly if JS on partner site doesn't need read — often still HttpOnly if server-side only)
